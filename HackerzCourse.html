First, let's learn the basics of hacking.

Hacking is often misunderstood, most people think that hacking is doing bad things on computers but that is not always true. Lets learn about white-hat hacking and black hat hacking. White-hat hacking is using your hacking skills for good like telling companies the security issues they have and how to fix them

Black-hat hacking is when you use it to make viruses or steal personal data. Black-hat hacking is a big NO NO!

Disclaimer! I do not condone any black hat hacking or getting personal info from people without the permission of that person. This website is for educational purposes only!

Beginner Learning Path Learn networking basics (IP addresses, DNS, HTTP) Understand operating systems (Linux is widely used) Study web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) Learn cybersecurity fundamentals Practice in safe environments (like labs or simulators)

Ethical hacking must be done only on systems you own or have explicit permission to test. Unauthorized access is illegal and can lead to serious consequences.
